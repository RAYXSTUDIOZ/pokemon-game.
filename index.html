How To Play ?


There are three legendry pokemons in your team and in your opponent team. there are four types of attacks which are different for different type of pokemons as per their type. you can choose one attack at time and you opponent team's pokemon will attack then. there is no any type of type advantage in this game. if you will win then you will be honoured by a trophy.
